Our History

We're a proud family of nut farmers from Northern California. What started as a small family farm in 1913 has grown into the global operation we are currently. Grandpa Dee (and Grandma Dee) just loved nuts. While caring for the very first tree, Grandpa Dee realized he just had a talent for caring for nuts.
